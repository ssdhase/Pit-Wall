Goal ETA
Log whenever you weigh in — daily, weekly, whenever. One entry per date (re-logging a date updates it). Use the date arrows at the top to backfill past weigh-ins.

How the prediction works: it averages your calorie deficit over the last 14 days (only counting days where you logged both food and burn), converts it to a loss rate using ~7,700 kcal per kg of fat, and projects when you'll cross your goal weight — shown as the dashed line on the chart. It's an estimate: real weight loss isn't perfectly linear (water, glycogen, adaptation), so treat the date as a direction, not a promise.

Profile — Auto Targets

Auto (from profile)
Manual
How auto mode works: maintenance = BMR (Mifflin-St Jeor) × your non-exercise activity level. Each day's intake target = maintenance + active calories you log that day − your deficit. So gym days earn more food at the same deficit. Macro targets: protein 2.0 g/kg bodyweight, fat 25% of calories, fiber 14 g per 1,000 kcal, carbs get the rest. Weight comes from your latest weigh-in (or the field above), so targets recalibrate as you lose.

Manual Targets

These apply only in Manual mode. In Auto mode the daily target and macros come from your profile. Goal weight is used by both modes for the Weight tab projection.
