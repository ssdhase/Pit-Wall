Goal ETA
Log whenever you weigh in — daily, weekly, whenever. One entry per date (re-logging a date updates it). Use the date arrows at the top to backfill past weigh-ins.

How the prediction works: it averages your calorie deficit over the last 14 days (only counting days where you logged both food and burn), converts it to a loss rate using ~7,700 kcal per kg of fat, and projects when you'll cross your goal weight — shown as the dashed line on the chart. It's an estimate: real weight loss isn't perfectly linear (water, glycogen, adaptation), so treat the date as a direction, not a promise.
